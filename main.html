What is QBoost?

QBoost is a free SoundCloud repost platform created by producers for producers. Supported by ScPlaner, users connect their SoundCloud account to QBoost and are then able to submit requests to have their music reposted over the entire network!

Once you're part of the network, you just send us an email and ask to reposted!

Why Join QBoost?

  • Its FREE!

  • More reach! - Get your music reposted to thousands of real people!

  • Most other networks require you to pay for reposts - Qboost is free!

  • No catch - you can disconnect your account at any time

  • Tracks submitted are vetted before being reposted across the network - Quality assured!

  • Channels in the network are vetted - No fake users or accounts, only real people!

  • Simple to use - Email us a repost request, get approved and get reposted!

  • Request up to 3 reposts a month!

Requirements

  • QBoost requires you to multi-link your account using SCplanner. This DOES NOT grant QBoost access you your account, however it does allow QBoost to repost tracks on your soundcloud page.

  • Have at least 1000 Soundcloud followers
